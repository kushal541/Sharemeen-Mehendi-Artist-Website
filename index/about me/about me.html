BIOGRAPHY

My name is Sharemeen Khan, and I am a Mauritian henna artist working at le Caudan Warterfront, Mauritius. I am someone who love drawing and painting using henna. It all begins at the age of 11 years old where I started to develop a passion for drawings. During my school time, I was always regular in my art classes and have learn a lot of things such as ART history, ART biographies, use of different mediums and techniques, and explores ART in our everyday lives. After leaving school, the only work I did which was not related to art was to work as a shop supervisor in a company in Port Louis for about one and a half year. After the company closed its door, I decided to focus myself on what I do the best which is art. I started to try for three months and saw that not only Mauritians people but also many tourists were interested in henna. Then finally it became my business.
